How to Calculate Sales Commission?

Whether you are in real estate, car sales, or a freelance affiliate marketer, knowing exactly how much you earn per sale is vital. Sales commission is simply a percentage of the total sale amount.

The formula is straightforward: Commission = Sale Amount × (Commission Rate / 100).

Example: If you sell a property for $200,000 and your agreed commission rate is 3%, your earnings would be $200,000 × 0.03 = $6,000. Use our free tool above to instantly calculate your payout!
