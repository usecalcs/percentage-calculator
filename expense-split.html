How to Split Group Expenses Fairly?

Whether you are organizing a road trip, renting a vacation house, or just splitting the cost of groceries with roommates, calculating who owes what is crucial for group harmony.

To find the equal share per person, simply divide the Total Group Cost by the Number of People involved. For example, if a weekend cabin costs $850 and there are 4 of you, the math is $850 / 4 = $212.50 per person.
