How to Quote a Freelance Project?

Pricing your freelance work correctly is crucial to avoid working for free when projects inevitably take longer than expected. Whether you are a web developer, designer, or consultant, this simple framework works:

  1. Estimate your hours: Be realistic about how long the project will take.
  2. Set your hourly rate: This should cover your time, taxes, and software expenses.
  3. Add a contingency margin: Always add 15% to 20% to cover unexpected client revisions, bugs, or scope creep.

Example: A 40-hour web design project at $25/hr is $1,000. Adding a 20% contingency margin ($200) brings your final safe quote to $1,200. Use our calculator above to quickly quote your next big client!
