How to find the Percentage of a Number?

Finding the percentage of a specific number is a common task for calculating tips, sales tax, or commissions. The math formula is straightforward:

Result = (Percentage / 100) × Total Value

Step-by-Step Example

Let's say you are at a restaurant and want to leave a 20% tip on a $85 bill.

  1. Divide the percentage by 100 to get the decimal format: 20 / 100 = 0.20.
  2. Multiply that decimal by the total value: 0.20 × 85 = 17.
